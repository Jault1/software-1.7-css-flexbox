About me

After working as a photographer for 10 years, I left to delve into the world of computers and the internet. I went to school for a well-rounded education in UNIX, programming, hardware, and computer networking. After getting my assiocate degree, I landed a job using html, css, & ASP with VB Script. That lastet about a year before my next job where I upgraded to MS .NET. After doing that for 3 years, I had an opportunity to make a lot more money, so I left there to go back into photography, this time as a business owner. After running that for 10 years and mostly raising my kids, I decided to get back into computers and I landed in a job doing email broadcasts for Adobe. That compnay shut down and now I'm at a cretive marketing agency where I have a lot of free-range to do what I want, how I want, and with the technologies I want to work with. I work along side a great creative guy who makes my code pretty.
